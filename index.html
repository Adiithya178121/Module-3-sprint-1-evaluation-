Underwater Contamination Monitoring

An underwater Robot also called ROUV is a form of a smaller size submarine that is controlled by human command. In this research, we are using ROV to collect water samples from different water bodies that are then processed to obtain results using 10T tools.
This ROV can also collect water samples from remote locations and deep inside the water bodies which is difficult to collect in normal Lab methods.
Lab-based water quality monitoring system takes time and difficult to collect water samples from remote places. Also, in recent times due to limited water resources, growing population, and pollution, providing purewater to all living organisms is difficult. Hence there is a great need for better techniques that measure the purity of water quality in real-time day to day life.
Remotely Operating Underwater Vehicle (ROUV) with water quality sensors controlled via Android app.
